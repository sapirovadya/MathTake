Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered here. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

MathTake is an online platform that makes learning math fun and interactive for children.

You can schedule a private lesson by logging into your account, navigating to the 'Schedule Lesson' section, and selecting a suitable date and time with your preferred teacher.

You can sign up for MathTake by clicking the 'Sign Up' button in the navigation bar and filling out the registration form.

Payments can be made through the 'Payments' section of the website. Enter your payment details and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the transaction.
